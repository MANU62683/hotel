About Iron Hill

Iron Hill is a landmark dining destination that combines exceptional cuisine, craft brewing expertise, and stunning architectural design to create an unparalleled experience for our guests.

Founded in 2007, our restaurant has quickly become renowned for our commitment to culinary excellence, featuring a diverse menu that draws inspiration from global cuisines while showcasing local ingredients and seasonal specialties.

What truly sets Iron Hill apart is our on-site craft brewery, where our skilled brewers create a rotating selection of distinctive beers that complement our food offerings perfectly. From hoppy IPAs to rich stouts and refreshing seasonal brews, there's something to satisfy every beer enthusiast.

Our award-winning architectural design creates a dynamic, multi-level dining experience with a variety of spaces to suit any occasion. From our spacious main dining room and intimate private dining areas to our stunning outdoor terrace with panoramic city views, each space offers its own unique ambiance.
